Adsider Conference: Landing Page

The 2-day conference for ad professionals in Kyiv that focused on valuable adtech insights and productive networking. 50+ top industry experts have covered the whole programmatic ecosystem and revealed new market opportunities in the CEE & MENA regions.
I worked with Adsider in the role of a UI/UX Designer. I have created and implemented the concept of a conference website, as well as developed creative branding materials.
