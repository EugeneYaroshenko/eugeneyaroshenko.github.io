GOGOLTRAIN: Art Project

StartUP GOGOLFest is one of the TOP-5 multidisciplinary festivals in Europe. It focuses on the creation of a modern cultural myth that unites different cities in Ukraine and Ukraine with the world.
Scarcity of transportation options to get to the festival, as well as lack of awareness about the event in Ukraine, refrain hundreds more from visiting the festival. Group of enthusiasts (including me) have approached the Ministry of Infrastructure of Ukraine to organize an additional unique train for festival participants.
