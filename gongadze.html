Georgiy Gongadze Award: Website

In 2019 a conceptually new journalism award emerged in Ukraine – the Georgiy Gongadze Award. The award is called to support the journalists who act on principles and values of independent journalism, demonstrate professionalism, expertise, and innovation.
In early 2020 I was approached by PEN Ukraine and KMBS Alumni to design a website for the award.

Website as a Storyteller

To build a bridge between the user and Georgiy Gongadze Award background, website tells a story of journalists that seek truth in the world of labyrinth and hurdles

Visual Differentiation

The website’s materials are divided into categories - Projects, Insights, and News. We have designed for every category a unique symbol and corresponding color for a better navigation and overall pleasing surfing experience.

Enhanced Reading Experience

Every story matters, and we have made sure that the user remains engaged with the content for the entire time spent reading.

Visual Differentiation

Georgiy Gongadze was a very influential journalist in Ukraine. He was killed in September 2000 by the ruling political elite. His name became a symbol of freedom and fight against oppression nation-wide. In contrast to this image, the website presents his life story in light of who he was as a person.

Mobile Version

On smaller screens, the website becomes responsively a simplified version of a desktop layout.
